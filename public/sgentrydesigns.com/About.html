Creating clothing that defines urban chic in a modern way, with art in mind, is empowering you to take the world by storm. My designs are a blend of hard and soft lines and materials. Durable fabrics juxtaposed against soft curves, which creates a dreamy, yet sophisticated detailed look that fits and flatters the human figure. Designed to inspire empower and engage the wearer in the every day format.


From an early age I have been exploring art, which has greatly influenced my designs. My interpretation of the world is most clearly expressed through visual media. Art is simply an expression of the world around us. It is shape, color, and form in conflict. Fashion is no different. It is also an art form, in fact, you could go as far as saying it's one of the most important art forms as art can also be defined as an expression of one's self. What are you doing every morning when you choose something from your closet to wear? You are expressing your self, even if it is in the most practical since of the verse.
